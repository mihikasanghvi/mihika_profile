Advancements in Land Use Land Cover Classification: Deep Learning and Sentinel-2 Satellite Imagery Integration

This study investigates deep learning for land use and land cover (LULC) classification using a Sentinel-2 dataset of over 213,750 pre-processed images across seven classes, employing six models (LinkNet, UNet, etc.) with accuracies ranging from 92% to 96%. The research aims to enhance automated LULC classification methodologies by comparing model performance and addressing challenges like data scarcity and class underrepresentation.
